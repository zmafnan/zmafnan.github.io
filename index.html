About Me

Hello, I'm Zikri!

I'm a passionate tech professional based in Bandung, Indonesia, currently working as a Lean Supervisor at PT Metro Pearl Indonesia.

With expertise in Machine Learning, Data Science, and Full-Stack Development, I bring a diverse skill set to solve complex problems and create innovative solutions.

In my current role in the Lean Department, I focus on manufacturing process improvement, production data analysis, and efficiency optimization. I also develop internal digital tools such as production monitoring systems, quality control applications, and data dashboards to support decision-making and operational efficiency.

I hold a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science from General Achmad Yani University and am constantly expanding my knowledge in the ever-evolving tech landscape.
